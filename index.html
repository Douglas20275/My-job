All the Automotive Services You Need, Under One Roof

At Sichey Automotive, we're your complete partner for all things vehicle-related. From routine maintenance to specialized services, our expert team is here to ensure your vehicles perform at their best.

Comprehensive Vehicle Maintenance

Keep your vehicles in top condition with our full range of maintenance services. From **routine checks** and **engine diagnostics** to **brake inspections** and **tire services**, we handle it all with precision and care.

Expert Oil Change & Fluid Services

Ensure your engine runs smoothly with our professional **oil changes** using premium lubricants. We also provide thorough **fluid checks and replacements** for transmission, coolant, power steering, and more.

Professional Vehicle Painting & Bodywork

Restore your vehicle's aesthetic appeal and structural integrity with our **top-notch spray painting**, **custom color matching**, and **expert bodywork repairs**. From minor dents to major collision restoration, we bring your vehicle back to life.

Genuine Spare Parts Supply

Access a comprehensive inventory of **genuine spare parts** for a wide range of vehicles, including **commercial vehicles**, **Solis tractors**, and **Solis service kits**. We ensure quality and authenticity for lasting performance.

Flexible Tractor Rentals

Need heavy-duty power without the commitment? Our **tractor rental services** offer flexible options for agricultural, construction, and other demanding tasks. Choose from a variety of models for short or long-term use.

Electric Vehicle Sales & Leasing

Step into the future with our range of **high-performance electric vehicles**. We offer flexible **purchasing and leasing options** to make sustainable transportation accessible for everyone.

Custom Fleet Solutions & Management

Optimize your business operations with our **bespoke fleet integration and management strategies**. We help maximize efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance the performance of your entire vehicle fleet.

EV Charging Infrastructure Development

Ready to embrace electric? We assist in the **planning, installation, and deployment of robust EV charging solutions** for homes, businesses, and public spaces, ensuring seamless adoption.

Expert Training & Consultation

Empower your team and make informed decisions with our **comprehensive training programs** and **strategic consultation services** on electric vehicle adoption, fleet optimization, and automotive technology.
